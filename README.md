## Overview
The CU Autonomous Racing website serves as the central hub for our collegiate autonomous racing team. It showcases our projects, team members, events, and ways to get involved. The site is designed to be **responsive, interactive, and easy to maintain** for a team of engineers.

---

## Key Features
- **Interactive Website:** Live updates, team dashboards, and dynamic content to keep members and visitors informed.  
- **Responsive Design:** Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.  
- **Team & Project Showcase:** Highlights AI, control systems, and machine learning projects.  
- **Event & Recruitment Management:** Displays upcoming workshops, competitions, and orientations.  
- **Git & Docker Integration:** Ensures consistent development workflows and reproducible deployments across environments.  
- **Cloud Deployment:** Hosted on **Vercel** with domain management through **Namecheap** for seamless public access.
